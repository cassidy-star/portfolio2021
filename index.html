My journey

20

The end of my school year.

After matriculating in 2017, I worked at Tekkie Town for 1 month during the December period. I was a cashier, working with the cash register and worked as sale assistant selling their products.

19

My first year in adult world.

In 2018 I joined an ICT Infrastructure course with Capaciti. It was a 1-year course of which the first 6 months was theory. The other 6-months was an internship. With the internship I worked for BCX at Woolworths Head Office. I helped with Firstline suport and IT support while being there. At the end i recieved a few certifications as well. One for the completion of the ICT infrastructure programme, Introduction to networks, IT essentials and Data Science.


18

The second year post high school.

In the year 2019 I joined a coding Bootcamp. Presented in partnership with Life Choices and Co-Grammer. This year I started to code as a beginner and learnt so much about programming.I really knew I would enjoy it. At Life Choices, I learnt a lot. We received soft skill classes and had such a diverse culture. Also towards the end recieved my Coding Bootcamp certification.

17

This was the year of covid.

At the end of the year i joined part of the YES 4 YOUTH programme. Which will also be a 1-year contract with six months working for LC Studio and the othe six months for another company.
